Registrar General


Shri Narendra Dutt


Shri Narendra Dutt S/o Late Shri Bhola Dutt (Retired Judge, District and Sessions Courts Delhi), Born on 18/10/1969. Initial Schooling till 10th Class was done from King George Royal Indian Military College now known as Chail Military School, Shimla Hills, Himachal Pradesh. Did Plus two (10+2) from summer Fields School, New Delhi. Passed B.A. (Pass) course from St. Stephenâ€™s College, Delhi University, in the year 1991. Passed LL.B from Campus Law centre Delhi University, in the year 1994. Thereafter, got enrolled with Bar Council of Delhi and practiced on Criminal side at District Courts Delhi and High Court of Delhi till 2008. In the year 2004-2005 contested Bar Association election and was elected as Additional Secretary of New Delhi Bar Association. In the year 2008, joined Uttarakhand Higher Judicial Service (direct recruitment) and the first posting was as Additional District Judge at Dehradun, where held the post of Special Judge C.B.I.(Anti Corruption) and Special Judge, Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances(NDPS). Remained there till June 2010 and thereafter was transferred to High Court of Uttarakhand, at Nainital as Registrar Judicial. Shri Narendra Dutt has been appointed as Registrar General, High Court of Uttarakhand, Nainital vide notification dated 29/07/2016 and assumed charge of the office on 01/08/2016. Sri Narendra Dutt is also having additional charge of Registrar (Vigilance) of High Court of Uttarakhand, vide notification number 216/UHC/Admin.A/2016, Dated July 29, 2016.
