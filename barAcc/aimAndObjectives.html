Aim & Objectives


> To submit and uphold the Constitution of India and to work in furtherance of its social objectives, Fundamental Rights and Directive Principles of State Policy and Human Rights.
> To protect and promote the interest of legal profession in Orissa in general and the members in particular.
> To assist the bar in upholding its rights and dignity in its relations with Executive and Judiciary.
> To have due regard to the philosophy of jurisprudence and encourage investigation, research and development of the same.
> To apply the knowledge and experience in the field of law to the promotion of public good.
> To set up a code of conduct, discipline and etiquette.
> To provide for itself a place for all its members to gather.
> To strive for dissipation of the knowledge and benefit of law accruing to the citizen of the land and for realization of such objectives to hold public educative seminars and discussions.
> To establish and maintain an up-to-date library "by constituting a charitable trust by the members".
> To take measures to provide for financial aid to the members of the Bar in strained circumstances and to the members of their family on their death.
> To organize cultural meetings and legal discussion.
> To ensure recreation and sports, both indoors and out door to its members.
> The management of the affairs of the association is entrusted in accordance with rules and regulations of the association to the Executive Committee.
