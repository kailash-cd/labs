Our Mission

A better informed individual can make better decision is what we believe in.

Sahi Raah is a bunch of enthusiastic and ambitious young folks who want to help students in picking up the correct path for their career. Majority of young kids who are in schools are so engrossed in their exams and homework that they either do not get time to think of their future prospect or they do not have correct guidance to help them understand the options they have except IITs, Medicine and law.
We have a very straightforward approach – Let kids decide what they want to do with their lives. To enable them for this arduous task, we provide them the necessary tools and guidance.
A quick chat with your neighbor’s son will make you realize how less informed kids are about their future options. We were in a similar stage, not very long back. We have struggled and now we want to pass on whatever experience we have to young minds.
We conduct seminars to help students understand that there are tons of options available and if one is not good in Maths, it is not the end of the world. We tell them about school level competitions and events they should attend to help them reach their future goals.
